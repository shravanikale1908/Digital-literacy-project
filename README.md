##Project Overview
This project is based on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, with the primary objective of helping peers understand and navigate the digital world effectively. It focuses on developing essential digital literacy skills, including creating a professional online presence, practicing safe internet usage, understanding digital communication, and recognizing potential cyber threats.

The project is structured into five modules, each targeting a specific aspect of digital literacy. All tasks and activities are organized within this GitHub repository, along with supporting files, relevant screenshots, and a comprehensive project report for reference.

Through this project, I gained hands-on experience with various online platforms and tools, which enhanced my technical skills and deepened my understanding of responsible and ethical digital behavior.

##  Module Summaries

###  Module 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
In this module, I designed a one-page infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and highlight its importance. The infographic covers key areas such as safe internet practices, the use of smart digital tools, and maintaining a professional online presence. This task enhanced my ability to present information in a clear, concise, and visually engaging manner, making it easier for others to understand essential digital concepts.

###  Module 2: Digital Portfolio
In this module, I created my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I learned how to present my skills and information in a proper way.
This will help me build a strong online presence and showcase my work in the future.

###  Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this module, I used HackerRank and Google Forms. I practiced a basic coding problem to improve my skills and created a digital literacy quiz. This helped me learn both coding and how to use online tools for collaboration and quizzes.

###  Module 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
In this module, I learned how to communicate professionally in the digital world by practicing formal emails for academic and professional purposes. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts. This helped me understand the importance of clear, respectful communication online and improved my overall communication etiquette.

###  Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness
In this module, I studied a cybercrime case and created a prevention checklist to understand online safety better. I learned about different types of digital threats and how they can affect users. I also explored ways to stay safe while using online platforms. This helped me become more aware and responsible when using the internet.
