## Task 1 - Brief

I used Canva to create a one-page infographic on digital literacy. The design covers three main topics: what digital literacy is, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. It explains how to use technology effectively, highlights tools like Google Docs and Canva, and provides safety tips such as not sharing passwords and avoiding suspicious links.

I found it interesting to arrange text and visuals creatively, but it was slightly challenging to keep the content short and well-organized. Overall, it was a useful and creative experience.
