Part B – Create a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:
16.
> Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use
* Think before you post anything
* Maintain a positive and respectful tone
* Protect your personal information (privacy settings)
* Use social media for learning and networking
* Verify information before sharing



> Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use
* Don’t share fake news or unverified content
* Don’t post offensive or inappropriate comments
* Don’t overshare personal details (phone number, address, etc.)
* Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
* Don’t waste excessive time on social media
