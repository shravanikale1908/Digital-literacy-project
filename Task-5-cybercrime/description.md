### Task-5-brief

Scams are effective because they exploit human emotions like urgency, fear, and temptation rather than relying on advanced technology. Even well-informed people can fall victim, especially in cases like “receive money” scams that misuse UPI PINs. 

The key takeaway is to stay cautious—avoid rushing, verify transactions, and think carefully before clicking links or making payments. Being mindful and taking time can help prevent online fraud.
