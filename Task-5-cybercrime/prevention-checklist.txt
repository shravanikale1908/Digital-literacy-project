Stay Safe Online – Checklist
*  Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts 
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email and banking 
* Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone 
* Always double-check UPI requests before approving payments (you don’t need a PIN to receive money) 
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links from unknown messages or emails 
* Keep your apps and phone software updated 
* Use only trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid public Wi-Fi for banking 
* Log out from accounts on shared or public devices 
* Be cautious of fake job offers, internship scams, or prize messages 
* Regularly check your bank transactions for unusual activity 
* Report any cyber fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) 
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud complaints
